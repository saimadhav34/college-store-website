About the Store

Welcome to Vaadevi College's official e-commerce stationary store! We offer a wide range of stationary products tailored for students, faculty, and staff. Whether you need notebooks, pens, folders, or other essential supplies, our store has everything you need to succeed academically.

Products We Offer

  • Notebooks and Journals
  • Pens, Pencils, and Markers
  • Folders and Binders
  • Art Supplies
  • Calculators and Geometry Sets
  • College Merchandise

How to Order

Ordering from our store is easy! Browse our online catalog, select the items you need, and proceed to checkout. We offer multiple payment options, and your items will be delivered directly to your doorstep or can be picked up from the college store.
